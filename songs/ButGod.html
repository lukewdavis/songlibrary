But God

Common Bond Quartet

[Verse 1]
The call comes in. We get the word
An accident, or something, has occurred.
We begin to pray, believing we are heard.
Doctors say, “Prepare for the worst.”

[Chorus]
But God, was first on the scene
Before it happened…He knew our need
How many of us would find it quite odd
That we wouldn’t be here, except for two words…But God.

[Verse 2]
In all our lives we seldom know
How many nights the angels were bent low.
They are never far. The Father’s always close.
Some may say, “Give up.” They don’t know…
